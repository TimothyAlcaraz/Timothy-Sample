Noli me Tangere Characters

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
-He is referred as Ibarra.
-He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra and he studied in Europe for seven years.
-He also knows languages like English and German language other than Tagalog.
Maria Clara
-She is the daughter of Padre Damaso.
-Maria Clara is a Roman Catholic who became humorless and prone to fainting.
-She is also Ibarra's fiancee.
Don Rafael Ibarra
-He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra.
-He is also the richest man in San Diego and his closest friends are Kapitan Tiago and Tenyente Guiverra.
Padre Damaso
-Padre Damaso is the main antagonist in Noli me Tangere.
-He also hates Crisostomo Ibarra because for him, people that study in a different country is making them arrogant.
Padre Sibyla
-He is a Dominican priest and former teacher of Crisostomo Ibarra.
